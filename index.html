What is Blue Carbon?

Blue carbon refers to carbon captured and stored in coastal and marine ecosystems such as mangroves, seagrass meadows, and salt marshes. These ecosystems are among the most carbon-dense habitats on Earth, sequestering carbon at rates up to 40 times faster than terrestrial forests.

Through Varenyam, firms can access verified blue carbon credits, contributing directly to climate action while supporting coastal communities and biodiversity conservation.
